About Boxsmith

Built on legacy. Built for founders.

Boxsmith is the D2C packaging arm of CDC Printers, a Kolkata print service provider with over two decades of manufacturing excellence and more than 120 national awards. We are amongst India's most sustainable print providers, and print only on FSC- or PEFC-certified paper.

We built Boxsmith because premium packaging was gatekept behind massive minimum orders and opaque processes. Growing brands deserved better. Premium boxes from just 200 units, a real prototype in 3 days, and a system-driven, fully transparent process — including a customer portal where you track your job live, with real-time updates at every stage.
